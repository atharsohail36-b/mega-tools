🔒 Privacy Policy

📅 Last Updated: April 2026 🌍 Version: 2.1
📦 Data Collection & Storage ▼

AbOmniTools operates entirely in your browser. No personal data is sent to external servers by default.

  • All calculations happen locally on your device
  • History, favorites & preferences stored via localStorage
  • No account registration required
  • No IP logging or fingerprinting

Exception: Optional features like Weather or QR Generator may send minimal query data to third-party APIs (see below).

🍪 Cookies & Tracking ▼

We do not use:

  • ❌ Tracking cookies or pixels
  • ❌ Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or similar
  • ❌ Third-party advertising networks
  • ❌ Cross-site tracking scripts

The only storage used is localStorage for your preferences, which you can clear anytime via browser settings or our Settings > Clear Data option.

🛡️ Your Data Rights (GDPR/CCPA) ▼

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: Export all your stored data anytime
  • Delete: Permanently remove all local data with one click
  • Portability: Download your data in machine-readable JSON format
  • Withdraw Consent: Change preferences anytime via Consent Manager

⚠️ Deleting data is permanent and cannot be undone. Export first if you want a backup.
